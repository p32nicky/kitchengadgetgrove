FAQ

Can guests take sourdough starters home easily?

Yes! A fed, active starter in a sealed jar is easy to transport. Include clear care instructions to help guests keep their starter alive.

Are sourdough wedding favors gluten-free?

While traditional sourdough uses wheat, you can opt for gluten-free flour blends and label accordingly. Many baking tools, like the Sourdough Whisk Set, are naturally gluten-free.

How should I store sourdough tools like the Sourdough Whisk Set?

Store tools in a dry place. For long-term storage, keep the lame in a protective case and hand-wipe the whisk and scraper after use.
