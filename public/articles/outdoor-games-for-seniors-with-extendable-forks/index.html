Setting Up a Safe Play Area

Safety first. Seniors appreciate clear boundaries and a flat surface. Here’s a quick checklist:

  1. Mark the play zone with soft, low‑height fencing or a line of cones.
  2. Use a smooth lawn or a padded deck to avoid tripping.
  3. Keep the forks within easy reach; never let someone hold it by the tip when it’s fully extended.
  4. Have a first‑aid kit nearby in case of minor bumps.
