Top Kid-Friendly Grilled Corn Recipes for Your Next Party

Keep it simple and fun with these flavor ideas:

  • Classic Butter & Salt: Brush with melted butter and sprinkle with a pinch of salt post-grill. Let kids add their own toppings.
  • Sweet & Spicy Glaze: Drizzle with a mix of honey and a dash of chili powder for a sweet-heat kick they’ll love.
  • Cheesy Corn: Sprinkle shredded cheddar over grilled corn and let it melt under the broiler before serving.

How to Grill Corn Like a Pro (Even with Kids Around)

Grilling corn doesn’t have to be complicated. Start by shucking the corn and soaking it in water for 15 minutes to prevent burning. Preheat the grill to medium-high and cook each side for 8–10 minutes. For a kid-friendly twist, let older kids help rotate the corn on the grill (with supervision, of course). FAQ

Can kids use the corn cob holders without supervision?

Yes! The silicone handles are designed to be easy for small hands to grip. However, always supervise kids near the grill or hot corn.

How do I grill corn without it drying out?

Soak the husked corn in water for 15 minutes before grilling. This adds moisture and prevents the kernels from becoming tough.
