Essential Steps to Grill Corn Like a Pro

Grilling perfect corn starts with the right prep. Follow these steps to make the most of your camping gear:
  1. Shuck and prep: Remove husks and silks, then brush with butter, oil, or your favorite seasoning.
  2. Secure the cobs: Slide each corn into a Girla Holder. The interlocking design keeps them organized in your cooler or on the grill.
  3. Grill over medium heat: Place holders directly on the grill. Close the lid for even cooking, turning every 5 minutes.

Maximize Flavor with These Grilling Tips

  • Add fresh herbs (like thyme or rosemary) to the butter for an extra layer of flavor.
  • Use indirect heat if your grill has hot spots to avoid burning the corn.
  • Soak the holders in water for 10 minutes before grilling to prevent sticking.
