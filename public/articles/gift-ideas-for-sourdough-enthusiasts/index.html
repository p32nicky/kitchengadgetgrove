FAQ

What makes a Danish dough whisk different from a regular whisk?

A Danish dough whisk uses thick, looped wires that gently fold ingredients together, preserving the dough’s structure and preventing over‑mixing. This is especially important for sourdough, where gluten development needs to be controlled.

Can a beginner use the Sourdough Whisk Set without prior experience?

Yes. The set is designed as a starter kit for baking beginners. The whisk helps incorporate starter evenly, the scraper aids in shaping and cleaning, and the lame provides a simple way to score loaves for a professional look.

How should I store the tools in the Sourdough Whisk Set to keep them in good condition?

Rinse each piece after use, dry thoroughly, and store them together in a dry drawer or the original pouch. Avoid leaving them in a damp environment, which can cause rust on the metal components.
