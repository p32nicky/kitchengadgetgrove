5 Easy Grilled Corn Toppings Kids Can’t Resist

Keep it simple and kid-approved with these grilled corn toppings for kids that add flavor without complexity:

  1. Classic Butter & Sugar: Melt butter over grilled corn and sprinkle with a pinch of sugar for a sweet, rich treat.
  2. Cheesy Pull-Apart: Sprinkle shredded cheddar or pepper jack over warm corn—it’ll melt into gooey, kid-friendly bits.
  3. Fruit Salsa: Mix diced mango, cilantro, lime juice, and a pinch of salt for a fresh, tangy twist.
  4. Honey & Cinnamon: Drizzle honey and sprinkle cinnamon for a caramel-like flavor kids adore.
  5. Mini Ranch Dip: Serve a small cup of ranch alongside the corn for easy dipping.
