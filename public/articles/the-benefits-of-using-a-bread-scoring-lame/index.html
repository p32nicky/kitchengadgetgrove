Tips for Mastering Bread Scoring Techniques

Ready to use your new lame? Start with these basics:
- **Angle matters**: Hold the blade at a 20-30 degree angle for most loaves; adjust based on dough tension.
- **Depth is key**: Shallow cuts (1/4 to 1/2 inch) work for most breads; deeper scores are for open-top loaves.
- **Practice patterns**: Try simple diagonal slashes for beginners or experiment with cloverleaf and wheat symbols as you gain confidence.
