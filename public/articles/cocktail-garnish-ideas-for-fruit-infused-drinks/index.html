Cocktail Garnish Ideas for Fruit-Infused Drinks

Here are some of the most compelling cocktail garnish ideas for fruit-infused drinks:

  • Edible flowers: Use edible flowers like lavender, hibiscus, or rose petals to add a touch of elegance and whimsy to your drinks.
  • Citrus wheels: Slice fresh citrus fruits like oranges, lemons, or limes to create beautiful garnishes that pair perfectly with fruit-infused cocktails.
  • Berries: Use fresh or frozen berries like raspberries, blueberries, or blackberries to create colorful and delicious garnishes.
  • Fresh herbs: Infuse your drinks with fresh herbs like mint, basil, or rosemary to create unique and refreshing flavor combinations.

Fruit-Infused Drink Garnish Tricks

When it comes to garnishing fruit-infused drinks, there are a few tricks you can use to take your creations to the next level:

  • Muddle your garnishes: Before adding your garnish to the drink, gently muddle it to release the flavors and oils.
  • Use a pick: Use a pick to secure your garnish to the glass or umbrella straw, making it easy to remove and replace.
  • Add a splash of citrus: Add a splash of citrus juice to your drink for an extra burst of flavor and aroma.
