Garden Helpers: Seedling Labels and Mini Shade

FAQ

Can I wash the mini umbrellas for reuse?

Because they’re made of thin paper, washing isn’t recommended. Instead, gently brush off crumbs with a dry cloth and store them in a dry place.

Are the sticks sturdy enough for heavier garnishes?

The sticks are designed for light garnishes like fruit wedges or small herbs. For heavier items, consider using a larger cocktail pick alongside the umbrella.

Do the umbrellas dissolve in water?

They will soften if left in water for an extended period, so it’s best to keep them dry until you’re ready to display them.
