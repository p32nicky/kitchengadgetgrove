Maintaining Tools on a Budget

Students need tools that last. The tools found in the 3-in-1 Sourdough Whisk Set are generally durable and easy to clean. To keep the Danish whisk and scraper in top shape, avoid putting them in the dishwasher if they have wooden handles, as the high heat can warp the wood or loosen the bond. A quick hand-wash with warm soapy water is all it takes.

For the scoring lame, remind the student to replace the blade occasionally. A dull blade tears the dough instead of slicing it, which can hinder the "spring" of the bread in the oven. Keeping the tools clean and sharp ensures that their hobby remains a joy rather than a chore.

No, you don't. In fact, many sourdough bakers prefer mixing by hand to feel the texture of the dough. A Danish dough whisk is specifically designed to handle the heavy lifting that a standard whisk cannot, making a stand mixer unnecessary for beginners.

How do I store a sourdough kit in a dorm?

Because the 3-in-1 Sourdough Whisk Set is compact, it can easily fit in a small kitchen drawer or even be hung on a command hook in the kitchen area. The scraper is slim enough to slide into almost any narrow gap.
