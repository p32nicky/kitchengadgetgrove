FAQ

Are umbrella straws truly biodegradable?

Most umbrella straws, including the 100‑pack featured here, are made from paper‑based materials that decompose in a commercial composting environment within 30‑60 days. They are a far better alternative to traditional plastic.

Can I use umbrella straws for hot beverages?

Umbrella straws are designed for cold drinks. The paper material can become soft and lose its shape when exposed to heat, so we recommend using them with chilled cocktails, iced teas, or water.

Do the parasols affect the taste of the drink?

No. The mini parasol is simply a decorative element that sits on the straw’s tip. It does not dissolve or leach any flavor, ensuring the beverage’s original taste remains untouched.
