Quick Grilled Corn Recipes for Dinner

With the right holders, you’ll want to try these two-minute recipes:

  1. Classic Herb Butter Corn: Brush shucked corn with melted butter, then sprinkle with garlic powder, paprika, and a pinch of sea salt. Grill for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally with the holders.
  2. Spicy Lime Corn: Rub cobs with olive oil and a mix of chili powder, lime zest, and cilantro. Grill as above, then drizzle with fresh lime juice post-grill.

Both recipes take under 15 minutes from start to finish, and the holders prevent the cobs from slipping or burning unevenly.

  • **Light dinners**: Add to salads or serve with zesty ceviche for a fresh twist.
  • **Kids’ meals**: Top with cheese or serve it with a honey-butter dip for a crowd-pleaser.

Time-Saving Tips for Perfect Corn Every Time

Prep ahead by shucking corn and storing it in airtight bags. Use the holders to keep cobs ready to grill without pre-skewering, and season in bulk. For extra flavor, wrap cobs in foil with herbs or butter while grilling to steam them gently. The holders’ interlocking design means you can stack them in drawers or on counters without tangling—a small detail that saves big time when hosting.
