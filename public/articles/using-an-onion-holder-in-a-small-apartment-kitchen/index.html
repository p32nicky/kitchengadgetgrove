Space-Saving Slicing: How to Use It

The key to using an onion holder in a small apartment kitchen is efficiency. Here’s a quick workflow:

  1. Stick the holder in the center of your cutting board or directly on the counter.
  2. Place the onion flat side down into the prongs. The anti-cut design keeps your fingers clear of the blade.
  3. Run your knife along the slicing guide for uniform cuts—no rocking or readjusting needed.

Since it doubles as a meat tenderizer (simply flip it over), you’re getting two tools in one without needing extra storage space.
