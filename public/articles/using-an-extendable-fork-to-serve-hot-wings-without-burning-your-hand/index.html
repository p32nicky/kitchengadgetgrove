How to Use an Extendable Fork to Serve Hot Wings

To use an extendable fork effectively, follow these simple steps:

  1. Position the fork at a comfortable distance from the hot wings.
  2. Extend the fork to the desired length, ensuring it's far enough away from your hand.

  4. Transfer the hot wings to the serving dish or plate.
