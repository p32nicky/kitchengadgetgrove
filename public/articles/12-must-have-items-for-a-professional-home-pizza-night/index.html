FAQ

What is the best way to store the wooden spurtle and dough stick?

Both pieces are naturally antibacterial and can be rinsed, then air‑dried. Store them together in a drawer lined with a paper towel to absorb moisture.

Can the vertical gyro pan replace a traditional pizza stone?

Yes. Its metal construction conducts heat quickly, and the vertical skewer allows airflow for a crisp crust, making it a solid alternative to a stone.

Are the umbrella straws safe for hot beverages?

The straws are designed for cold drinks; for hot beverages, stick to regular paper straws or remove the parasol to avoid melting.
