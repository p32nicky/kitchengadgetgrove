3 Grilled Corn and Sausage Recipes to Try Tonight

Pair the holders with these recipes to level up your grill game:

1. Classic Butter-Baked Corn with Spiced Sausages

Grill sausages over indirect heat while corn roasts on the Girla Holders. Brush the corn with melted butter, sea salt, and chili flakes. Serve the sausages sliced and paired with the charred cobs.

2. Maple-Glazed Sausages and Herb Corn

Brush sausages with a maple-mustard glaze during the last 5 minutes of grilling. For the corn, mix softened butter with fresh herbs like parsley and dill before slathering it onto the cob.

3. Spicy Korean-Style Sausage and Corn Skewers

Marinate sausages in gochujang, garlic, and soy sauce. Thread them onto the same skewers as the corn (using the holders for stability) and grill until golden. Serve with a sesame-ginger dipping sauce.

Tips for Grilling Success

Even with the best tools, technique matters. Soak corn in water for 10 minutes before grilling to prevent burning. For sausages, avoid piercing the casings to keep the juices inside. Use the Girla Holders to hold corn upright and rotate them every 2–3 minutes for even charring. Pair bold flavors: smoky paprika, lime zest, or cotija cheese all work beautifully.
