Set Up Your Frozen Margarita Bar Mini Umbrella Station

Designate a clean, spacious area and arrange the following:

  • Prepared frozen margarita ingredients in labeled containers.
  • A self-serve syrup station with flavors like mango, pineapple, or chili-lime.
  • The 150-pack of cocktail umbrellas in a visible, easy-to-grab bowl or tray.
  • Glasses on a rack with fresh lime wedges and sea salt for rimming.

Use a tablecloth or paper lanterns to add a tropical flair.

Craft the Perfect Frozen Margarita

Follow this quick recipe for crowd-pleasing drinks:

  1. Blend 1.5 oz tequila, 1 oz triple sec, 2 oz lime juice, and 3 oz margarita mix with ice until smooth.
  2. Taste and adjust sweetness using the syrup station.
  3. Rim glasses with salt and lime juice before serving.

Pro tip: Pre-blend large batches in a personal blender pitcher for faster service.

Presentation Tips for Your Frozen Margarita Bar Mini Umbrella

Serve and Enjoy Your Tropical-Themed Party Drinks
