How to Prep Jumbo Shrimp Skewers for the Gyro Pan

Start with 21/25-count jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined. Thread them onto metal skewers with alternating slices of bell pepper or cherry tomatoes for color and contrast. The Gyro Pan’s vertical setup means skewers must be evenly spaced to rotate smoothly. Secure skewers onto the pan’s central rod, ensuring they’re balanced to prevent wobbling during cooking. (Tip: Brush shrimp with olive oil after threading for a crispier finish.)

Mastering the Gyro Pan for Wedding Shrimp Skewers

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) with the Gyro Pan inside.
  2. Lightly oil the pan’s interior to prevent sticking.
  3. Set skewers on the pan, rotate the skewer rod to lock them in place.
  4. Roast for 12–15 minutes, turning the pan occasionally for even charring.
  5. Brush with a garlic-lime glaze or marinade during the last 3 minutes for bold flavor.
The Gyro Pan’s vertical skewering allows fat to drip away, avoiding greasy shrimp. For weddings, consider doubling the recipe—this method scales well in larger ovens or with multiple pans.

Serving Wedding Shrimp Skewers Like a Pro

Plating is key for weddings. Use the Gyro Pan’s rotating base as a serving station: place skewers back on it with a tray of aioli or mango salsa on the side. For a refined touch, serve on cedar boards or in towered glass containers with fresh herbs. Pair with crusty bread or pita for a shareable starter. Wedding Shrimp Skewers Gyro Pan: FAQs

Can I use the Gyro Pan for shrimp without it sticking?

Yes. The stainless steel interior heats evenly—just make sure to oil the skewers and pan before cooking. Avoid overcrowding the pan for optimal airflow.

How do I prevent the shrimp from overcooking?

Jumbo shrimp cook fast. Keep an eye on the first skewer; once it turns pink and curls, check the rest. Rotate the pan manually if parts are browning too quickly.

Is the Gyro Pan safe for high-heat oven use?

Absolutely. The pan is rated for up to 500°F (260°C), making it ideal for searing or slow-roasting. The skewer rod is removable for easy cleaning in the dishwasher.
