Safety Tips: Keep the Skits Harmless and Fun

While the idea is playful, it’s important to avoid actual food theft or dangerous situations:

  • Never force a fork into a guest’s plate. Use the extendable fork’s telescoping feature to reach without pushing.
  • Keep the fork’s tip away from sharp edges on plates or utensils to prevent accidental cuts.
  • Inform guests about the prank after the joke to ensure they don’t feel cheated or upset.
  • Use the fork only on non‑slippery surfaces to avoid it sliding off the plate.

Following these guidelines keeps the atmosphere light and the laughs safe.
