Three Refreshing Recipes for Your Next Batch

Now that you have the straws handled, you need a liquid vehicle worthy of such a tropical accessory. Here are three iced tea recipes that pair perfectly with the kitschy charm of a mini umbrella.

  1. Tropical Hibiscus Sunrise:
    Brew a strong concentrate of hibiscus tea and let it cool completely. In a pitcher, mix the hibiscus tea with pineapple juice and a splash of lime juice. The deep red color looks striking against the bright parasols. Serve over crushed ice to keep it instantly cold.
  2. Mango Green Tea Refresher:
    Steep Japanese green tea and chill it. Puree fresh mango chunks with a little water and simple syrup to create a mango slurry. Layer the mango puree at the bottom of the glass and slowly pour the green tea over the top to create a gradient effect. The green tea provides a grassy note that cuts through the sweet mango.
  3. Classic Southern Peach Tea with a Twist:
    Make your favorite black tea base. Instead of just adding slices of peach, muddle fresh peaches with mint and a bit of brown sugar at the bottom of the serving glasses. Top with the tea and ice. The minty, sweet aroma complements the tropical look of the umbrella straws perfectly.
