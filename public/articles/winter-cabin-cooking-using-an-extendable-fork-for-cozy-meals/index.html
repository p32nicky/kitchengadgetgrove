Tips for Maintaining Your Telescopic Forks in a Rustic Setting

Cabin kitchens can be humid, and metal can tarnish if not cared for. Follow these simple steps to keep your extendable forks looking like new:

  • Rinse immediately after use to remove food particles that could cause staining.
  • Dry thoroughly, especially the sliding sections, before storing.
  • Apply a light coat of food‑grade oil once a month if the forks sit unused for long periods.
  • Inspect the telescoping mechanism for smooth operation; a quick wipe with a damp cloth will prevent grit buildup.
