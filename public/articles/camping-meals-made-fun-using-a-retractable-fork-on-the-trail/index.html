Safety Tips for Using a Telescoping Fork Outdoors

  • Check the mechanism. Before each use, ensure the extension lock clicks firmly. A loose joint can snap back unexpectedly.
  • Watch your angle. When extending, keep the fork perpendicular to your body to avoid accidental strikes.
  • Clean after cooking. Food residue can attract insects; rinse the fork thoroughly before storing.
  • Store in a dry place. Even stainless steel can develop water spots; a quick wipe keeps it bright.
