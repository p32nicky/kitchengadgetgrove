FAQ

How do I keep my BBQ prep organized?

The best way to stay organized is to use a prep checklist and a "mise en place" approach. Chop all vegetables and portion your meats into separate containers before you ever start the grill. This prevents the chaos of trying to slice an onion while the burgers are burning.

Are corn holders really necessary?

While not strictly necessary, they are a huge safety and convenience win. They prevent guests from burning their hands and keep the corn stable while they apply butter or seasoning.

What is a spurtle used for in baking?

A spurtle is a traditional Scottish tool used for stirring soups and batters. In sourdough baking, its thin profile makes it ideal for mixing starters and scraping the curved edges of jars, making it more efficient than a bulky spoon.
