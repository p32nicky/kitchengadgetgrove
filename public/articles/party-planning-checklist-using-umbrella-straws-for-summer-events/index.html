Designate a central bar or a self‑serve drink table.   3. A small trash bin for used straws—these are disposable, so guests can toss them without worrying about cleaning.

Can I reuse the umbrella straws for multiple drinks?

They are intended as disposable items. Reusing them could transfer flavors and compromise hygiene, especially at larger gatherings.

Do the umbrella straws work with hot beverages?

They are best suited for cold drinks. The mini parasol may wilt or detach when exposed to heat, and the plastic could soften.

How many umbrella straws should I order for a 30‑guest party?

Assuming each guest will have two drinks, a 100‑Pack Umbrella Straws provides a comfortable buffer. You’ll have extras for décor and any unexpected refills.
