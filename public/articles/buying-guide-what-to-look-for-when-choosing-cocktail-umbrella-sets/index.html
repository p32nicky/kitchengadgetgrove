FAQ

Do cocktail umbrellas need to be waterproof?

Not necessarily. A light water‑resistant coating is enough to keep them from soaking through, but they’re designed for brief exposure. For longer‑lasting drinks, consider using a small paper towel underneath.

Can I reuse these paper umbrellas?

They’re best used once. The paper can become soggy or tear after a single use, so it’s safer to treat them as disposable to maintain a clean look.

Are these umbrellas safe for kids at parties?

Yes. The sticks are short and the paper is non‑toxic. Just make sure small children don’t chew on the sticks to avoid choking hazards.
