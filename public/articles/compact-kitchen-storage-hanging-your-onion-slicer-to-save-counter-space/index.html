Maintaining Your Stainless Steel Slicers

Once you've mastered how to store your onion slicer in a small kitchen, you need to make sure the tool stays in top shape. Stainless steel is incredibly durable, but it can still develop spots if left to air dry with mineral-heavy water.

After using your slicing guide, give it a quick scrub with a dish brush to get between the prongs. Pat it dry with a microfiber cloth before hanging it back on your magnetic strip or hook. This prevents water spots and ensures that the tool is hygienic and ready for your next meal prep session.

FAQ

Will a magnetic strip work for any onion slicer?

Only if the tool is made of a ferrous metal like stainless steel. The Onion Holder & Slicing Guide (2 PCS) is stainless steel, making it a perfect candidate for magnetic storage.

Can I hang these tools near the stove?

Yes, hanging them near the stove is convenient, but ensure they are far enough away to avoid grease splatter. If you hang them in a high-grease area, just give them a quick wipe-down once a week.
