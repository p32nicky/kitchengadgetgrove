Maintenance Tips for Your Prank Gear

To keep your jokes landing, you need your gear in top shape. Since these are telescopic, food particles can sometimes get trapped in the joints. After a party, make sure to fully extend the fork and rinse it under warm water with a bit of dish soap.

Avoid putting these in the dishwasher, as the high heat and harsh detergents can sometimes affect the sliding mechanism of the telescoping sections. A quick hand wash ensures that the fork glides smoothly the next time you need to steal a fry from across the table.

FAQ

Are extendable forks safe to use with real food?

Yes, as long as they are made of food-grade stainless steel. Always wash your prank forks before using them for the first time to remove any manufacturing residue.

How do I stop the fork from extending accidentally?

The extension usually happens when the fork is pushed or when the weight of the food pulls it. To keep it retracted, hold the handle firmly and avoid putting too much downward pressure on the tines until you are ready for the prank.

Can I use these for actual dining?

While they are functional forks, they are designed primarily for jokes. Because they are telescopic, they aren't as rigid as a standard dinner fork, so they are best used for soft foods and appetizers rather than heavy steaks.
