Step-by-Step Parasol Placement

Follow these steps to integrate mini parasols seamlessly:

  1. Arrange Base Items: Place heavier items like cheese wedges and meat slices first, leaving space for dips.
  2. Insert Umbrellas: Stick parasols into melon balls, grapes, or fruit skewers. For dips, insert them into the side of the ramekin.
  3. Balance Colors: Use the three umbrella styles to complement your color scheme. Place bright parasols near neutral meats and cheeses.
  4. Add Themed Touches: Pair with tropical fruits like pineapple or mango for a cohesive look.

Each umbrella’s paper parasol is sturdy enough to stay upright but delicate enough to avoid overpowersing the board.
