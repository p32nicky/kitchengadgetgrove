Essential Tips for Grilling Corn and Seafood Like a Pro

1. **Prep corn properly**: Remove silk and leaves, then soak in water for 15-20 minutes to prevent burning. The Girla Corn Holders let you skip wrapping in foil—just slide them on and grill straight.

2. **Oil the grill, not the food**: Brush grates with oil, not the corn or seafood, to avoid flare-ups. Direct heat gives that perfect char.

3. **Seafood tricks**: Shrimp cook fast—keep them on 2-3 minutes per side. For salmon or fish, wrap in parchment with herbs for a moist finish.

3 Grilled Corn and Seafood Recipes to Try This Summer

1. Grilled Corn and Shrimp Skewers

  • Slip corn onto Girla Holders and brush with butter.
  • Thread shrimp onto skewers with garlic, lime, and chili.
  • Grill corn 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally; shrimp need 2-3 minutes per side.

2. Seafood Corn Salad

Combine grilled corn, chopped shrimp, avocado, and red onion. Toss with lime juice, cilantro, and a drizzle of olive oil. Serve chilled.

3. Grilled Corn and Fish Tacos

Place white fish (like tilapia) and corn onto Girla Holders. Brush with taco seasoning. Warm tortillas and top with slaw and lime crema.
