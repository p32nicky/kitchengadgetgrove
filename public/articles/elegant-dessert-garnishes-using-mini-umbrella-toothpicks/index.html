How to Use Them Without Making a Mess

To avoid squishing delicate desserts, follow these tips:

  1. Insert the pick at a 45-degree angle for stability without poking through.
  2. Pair with small weights, like a chocolate-covered cherry, to keep them upright.
  3. For soft desserts (mousse, cheesecake), secure the pick in a base layer first.
