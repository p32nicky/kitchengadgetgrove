How to Use an Onion Holder for Faster Prep

If you've never used a prong-style slicer, the process is intuitive. You simply press the stainless steel prongs into the vegetable, creating a steady anchor. The handles then act as a physical barrier between the blade of your knife and your skin.

Here is the most efficient way to use this tool during your weekly prep:

  • Step 1: Cut the onion in half lengthwise to create a flat surface.
  • Step 2: Press the Onion Holder & Slicing Guide firmly into the center of the onion.
  • Step 3: Slide your knife between the prongs. The prongs act as a guide, keeping the knife on a straight path.
  • Step 4: Move the holder over by a fraction of an inch and slice again.

This method eliminates the "wobble" that happens when you try to slice a round object. Because the tool holds the vegetable steady, you can move much faster without sacrificing safety.
