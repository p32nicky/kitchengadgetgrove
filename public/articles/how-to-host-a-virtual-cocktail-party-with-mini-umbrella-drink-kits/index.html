Simple Recipes That Look Spectacular

Try to ship the boxes about one week before the party date. This ensures that even if there are minor postal delays, your guests will receive their packages and have time to buy the fresh ingredients or spirits you requested.

Do these umbrellas fit in standard glassware?

Yes, the toothpicks included in the 150-Pack set are standard size. They fit comfortably in rocks glasses, highball glasses, and even champagne flutes, making them versatile for whatever drink recipe you choose.

Can I use the picks for food if I don't make cocktails?
