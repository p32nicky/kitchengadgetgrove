10 Fun Umbrella Themed Drink Games

  • Parasol Plinko: Tape a large paper umbrella to a wall and let players drop mini umbrellas from the top. Score points based on where they land.
  • Umbrella Roulette: Stick an umbrella into a drink, then spin a bottle to pick someone to guess the color or pattern. Wrong? They drink!
  • Tropical Trivia: Host a Hawaiian or tiki-themed quiz. Award mini parasols as rewards for correct answers.
  • Drink Decoration Duel: Split into teams. First to decorate a drink with 5 umbrellas (plus fruit) wins. Use the 150-pack’s 3 styles for variety.
  • Lost at Sea: Blindfold someone and have them find a hidden umbrella in a box of drinks. If they pick the right one, they’re safe from dares.

FAQ

Can I reuse the drink umbrellas?

Paper parasols aren’t meant for reuse, but you can snap them off and recycle them after the party.

How do I secure umbrellas in drinks without them falling out?

Push the stick all the way into the fruit or rim of the glass—firm pressure keeps them upright.

Are these safe for glass bottles?

Absolutely. The picks are soft-tipped and won’t crack glass or plastic containers.
