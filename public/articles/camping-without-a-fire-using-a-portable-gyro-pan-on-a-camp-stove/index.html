How to Set Up Your Portable Gyro Pan on a Camp Stove

Follow these steps for camp stove compatibility:

  1. Mount the Gyro Pan securely on a sturdy tripod or camp stove stand.
  2. Preheat the pan over medium heat for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Load the skewer with stacked meat slices (turkey, beef, or chicken work well).
  4. Slow-roast for 20-30 minutes, rotating occasionally for even cooking.

Pro tip: Use a meat thermometer to check for doneness—this is key when cooking vertically on a camp stove.

FAQ

Can the Gyro Pan be used on any camp stove?

Yes, as long as the stove has enough height clearance and stable mounting options. Propane and butane stoves work best.

How long does meat take to cook on a camp stove gyro pan?

Plan for 20-40 minutes depending on thickness and heat level. Thin cuts cook faster, while thicker cuts need slower roasting.

Is the Gyro Pan lightweight for backpacking?

At 2.5 lbs, it’s not ideal for ultralight backpacking but perfect for car camping or basecamp setups.
