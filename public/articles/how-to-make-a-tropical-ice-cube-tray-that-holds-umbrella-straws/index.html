DIY Tropical Ice Cube Tray Setup

You don’t need a special tray to start. Here’s how to make one with everyday items:

  1. Choose a standard silicone ice cube tray (8-12 cavities).
  2. Insert an umbrella straw into each cavity, leaving the parasol above the rim.
  3. Pour your favorite juice, flavored water, or cocktail into the tray.
  4. Freeze for 6-8 hours.

When ready to use, pop the frozen cubes into a glass—the straw stays embedded, and the ice keeps your drink cold while adding a tropical vibe.
