How to Maximize the Life of Your Dishwasher Safe BBQ Gadgets

Even the best dishwasher safe tools need a little care to stay in top shape. Follow these simple steps to extend their life:

  1. Pre‑Rinse Quickly: Remove large food particles before loading them into the dishwasher. This helps prevent residue buildup.
  2. Use a Gentle Cycle: A standard or eco cycle is usually sufficient. Avoid harsh high‑heat cycles that could warp silicone.
  3. Check for Loose Parts: Inspect the gadgets after each wash to ensure no parts have come loose. Tighten or replace as needed.
  4. Store Upright: When not in use, keep the holders and skewers in a vertical position to prevent warping.
  5. Inspect for Wear: Replace any part that shows signs of cracking or rust to maintain hygiene.

FAQ

Can I put all my bbq gadgets in the dishwasher?

Only the ones labeled as dishwasher safe. Some metal tools with plastic or rubber parts may not withstand high heat. Check the manufacturer’s instructions before loading.

Do dishwasher safe gadgets lose their shape over time?

High‑quality silicone and stainless steel should retain their shape for years, even with regular dishwasher use. If you notice warping, it’s likely due to a faulty product or a very hot cycle.

What’s the best way to clean the silicone handles on the corn holders?

Rinse them with warm water and a mild dish soap before placing them in the dishwasher. The silicone stays soft and retains its bright color with minimal effort.
