How to Use and Store Your Interlocking Holders

1. Pre‑heat your grill or grill pan. While the heat is building, assemble the holders by sliding each pair of skewers into the slots. The interlocking design keeps them upright.

2. Place the holders on the grill. The silicone grips keep the holders in place even if you’re moving them around for even cooking.

3. Cook to perfection. Rotate the corn every few minutes for even browning.

4. Cool before storage. Once cooked, let the holders cool a bit before removing the corn. This prevents the silicone from warping.

5. Store in the pantry. The compact, interlocking shape means you can stack them in a small drawer or a kitchen bin without taking up much space.

Cleaning Tips for Longevity

Even though the Girla holders are dishwasher safe, a quick rinse with warm soapy water after each use keeps them looking bright. If you’re in a hurry, simply wipe the silicone handles with a damp cloth. Avoid abrasive sponges that could scratch the plastic.

For a deeper clean, soak the set in a bowl of hot water and a splash of vinegar for 15 minutes. This helps remove any lingering corn oils and keeps the holders odor‑free.
