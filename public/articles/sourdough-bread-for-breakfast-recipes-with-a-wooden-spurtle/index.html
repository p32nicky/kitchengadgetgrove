FAQ

Is the wooden spurtle safe for sourdough starters?

Absolutely. The non-reactive wood won’t interfere with fermentation, and the smooth finish prevents bacteria from clinging to the tool.

Can I use the spurtle for other breakfast batters?

Yes! Its design works well for thick pancake mixtures, Greek yogurt parfaits, or even blending smoothie ingredients with the dough whisk.

How do I clean the wooden spurtle?

Rinse with warm water and a mild soap, then dry immediately. Avoid soaking or using abrasive sponges to prolong the wood’s lifespan.
