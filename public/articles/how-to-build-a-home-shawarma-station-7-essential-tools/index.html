FAQ

Can I use a regular oven for shawarma?

Yes! The Vertical Gyro Pan works in most ovens. Set the temperature to 350°F and rotate the skewer manually every 20–30 minutes for even cooking.

How do I keep meat from drying out?

Marinate for at least 4 hours, use a meat thermometer (165°F for chicken, 145°F for beef/lamb), and let it rest 10 minutes before slicing.

What’s best to serve with shawarma?

Classic options include pita, rice, or salads. For drinks, pair with mint lemonade or iced tea. Use your umbrella straws for a festive touch.
