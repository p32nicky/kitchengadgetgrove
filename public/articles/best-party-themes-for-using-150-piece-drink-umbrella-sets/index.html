How to Keep Your Umbrellas Looking Fresh All Night

  • Store in a dry place until the event starts; humidity can cause the paper to curl.
  • Pre‑soak in a light citrus solution (1 tbsp lime juice per cup water) for 5 minutes, then pat dry. This adds a subtle shine and prevents sogginess.
  • Use a shallow dish of water with a few ice cubes to keep the umbrellas upright on the bar.
  • Rotate the umbrellas every hour; replace any that start to droop with fresh ones from the pack.

FAQ

Can I reuse the drink umbrellas for other decorations?

Yes. The paper parasols are sturdy enough to be repurposed for dessert garnishes, fruit platters, or even as place‑card holders for a themed dinner.

Are the umbrellas safe for hot beverages?

They are designed for cold or room‑temperature drinks. For hot beverages, place the umbrella on the rim rather than immersing it, to avoid paper weakening.

How many umbrellas should I allocate per guest?

Plan for 1‑2 umbrellas per drink. With a 150‑piece pack, you can comfortably serve up to 60 guests with two drinks each, plus a few extra for food garnishes.
