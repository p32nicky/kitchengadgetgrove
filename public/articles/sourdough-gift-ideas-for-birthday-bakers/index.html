FAQ

What makes a Danish dough whisk better than a regular whisk?

A Danish dough whisk has long, flexible wires that gently incorporate air into thick, sticky dough without over‑working the gluten. This results in a more open crumb and a healthier starter culture.

Can the Sourdough Whisk Set be used for non‑sourdough breads?

Absolutely. The whisk and scraper are versatile enough for any high‑hydration dough, from focaccia to ciabatta, while the lame is perfect for scoring artisan loaves of any style.

Is the scoring lame safe for beginners?

Yes. The lame’s single‑edge blade is sharp but easy to control. The included guide explains proper handling and scoring techniques, making it safe for anyone new to bread baking.
