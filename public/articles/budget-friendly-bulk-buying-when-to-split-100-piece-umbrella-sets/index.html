FAQ

How can I split a 100-piece umbrella set with friends?

Divide the pack into equal portions after counting the pieces. For odd numbers, rotate who gets the extra umbrella next time you share a bulk purchase.

How should I store leftover umbrellas?

Keep them in a sealed container away from heat. The straws work well in caddies or grouped with other disposable party supplies for easy access.

When isn’t bulk buying 100-piece sets worth it?

If you use fewer than 5 umbrellas yearly, splitting a set with others is better than buying a full pack yourself. Storage limitations or infrequent use also make smaller purchases more practical.
