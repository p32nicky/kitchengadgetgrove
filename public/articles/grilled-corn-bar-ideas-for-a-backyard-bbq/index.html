Grilled Corn Bar Ideas: Recipes to Inspire You

Here are some creative grilled corn recipes that are perfect for a BBQ:

Lime-Cilantro Grilled Corn: Grill corn ears until lightly charred, then brush with a mixture of lime juice, olive oil, and chopped cilantro.

Spicy Grilled Corn: Grill corn ears until lightly charred, then brush with a mixture of chili powder, lime juice, and olive oil.

Herb and Garlic Grilled Corn: Grill corn ears until lightly charred, then brush with a mixture of olive oil, minced garlic, and chopped fresh herbs like parsley or chives.
