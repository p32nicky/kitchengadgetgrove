How to Package the Spurtles for a Polished Presentation

Presentation matters, but you don’t need to spend a fortune. Here are three low‑effort packaging ideas that look upscale:

  1. Mini kraft boxes: Slip each spurtle into a 2×2‑inch kraft box, add a custom label with the couple’s names and wedding date, and seal with a twine bow.
  2. Glass jar favors: Place the spurtle inside a small mason jar with a pinch of sea salt or a tiny sachet of dried herbs. The jar can later be repurposed for pantry storage.

Regardless of the method you choose, include a short, printed note that explains how to use the spurtle with a sourdough starter. A quick tip like “Stir your starter twice daily with the wooden spurtle for an even, happy culture” makes the favor both beautiful and useful.

FAQ

Can the wooden spurtle be washed in the dishwasher?

No. To preserve the wood’s integrity and avoid warping, hand‑wash the spurtle with warm, soapy water and dry it promptly. A quick rub with a food‑safe oil once a month helps maintain its finish.

Is the spurtle safe for high‑heat cooking?

Yes. The wood is heat‑resistant up to about 350°F (177°C), making it safe for stirring hot mixtures, but it should not be left in a pan on direct flame for extended periods.
