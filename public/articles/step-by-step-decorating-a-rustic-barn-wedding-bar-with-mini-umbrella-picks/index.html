Step 1: Plan Your Wedding Bar Layout

Before decorating your wedding bar, take some time to plan the layout. Consider the size of your bar, the number of guests, and the type of drinks you'll be serving. You may want to create a few different stations or areas within the bar to keep things organized and make it easier for guests to navigate. Make sure to leave some space for your mini umbrella picks and other decorations.

Step 2: Add a Focal Point with Greenery

Greenery, such as eucalyptus or ferns, can add a beautiful touch to your wedding bar. Consider creating a focal point with a large piece of greenery or a few smaller bunches placed in a decorative vase or container. This will help draw the eye to the bar and create a sense of depth and interest. You can also use greenery to create a dramatic backdrop for your wedding bar.

Step 3: Add Mini Umbrella Picks to Your Drinks

Now it's time to add the mini umbrella picks to your drinks. Simply insert the pick into each glass or pitcher, making sure they're secure and won't fall out.
