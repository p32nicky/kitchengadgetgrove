FAQ

Can I use these straws for non-alcoholic drinks?

Absolutely. The 100-Pack Umbrella Straws are perfect for mocktails, sparkling waters, or kid-friendly punch. They add a festive touch to any beverage, regardless of the alcohol content.

What is the best glass for a tiki cocktail?

While traditional tiki mugs are great, any tall glass or hurricane glass works well. The goal is to have enough room for plenty of crushed ice and a decorative umbrella straw poking out the top.

How do I prevent my umbrella straw from sinking?

The trick is to fill your glass with plenty of crushed ice. The ice acts as a stabilizer, holding the straw and the mini parasol firmly in place so it doesn't tip over or slide to the bottom of the drink.
