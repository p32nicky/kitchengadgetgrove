FAQ

Is the retractable fork safe for everyday use?

It’s designed for pranks, so use it sparingly. The mechanism is sturdy, but frequent extension can wear the threads over time. For everyday dining, stick to a regular fork.

Can I use the fork with hot dishes?

Yes, the stainless steel can handle heat, but avoid touching the fork’s metal shaft directly with your hand after it’s been in a hot dish; use a towel or a fork cover if needed.

What if the fork doesn’t extend properly?

Check for debris in the telescoping tube. If it’s still stuck, gently tap the shaft or apply light pressure from both ends. If the problem persists, contact the manufacturer for a replacement or repair guide.
