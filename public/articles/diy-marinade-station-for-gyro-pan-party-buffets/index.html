How to Set Up Your DIY Marinade Station

1. **Choose Your Base**: Use a large tray or cooling rack to hold marinating meat and catch drips. Stainless steel trays work best for easy cleaning.
2. **Skewer Storage**: Keep extra skewers organized in a vertical stand. The Vertical Gyro Pan with Skewer for Oven doubles as a skewer holder when not in use.
3. **Marinade Jars**: Label jars for each recipe (e.g., lemon-herb for gyros, chipotle for al pastor). Use glass containers for visibility.
4. **Tongs & Gloves**: Keep tongs for handling skewers and gloves for food safety within arm’s reach.

Using the Vertical Gyro Pan for Perfect Results

Once your meat is marinated and loaded onto the skewer, the Vertical Gyro Pan with Skewer for Oven ensures even cooking. Here’s how to use it:
- **Secure the Skewer**: Thread marinated meat onto the skewer, securing with the pan’s built-in clamp.
- **Oven Setup**: Place the pan on the middle rack of a preheated oven (375°F/190°C) for steady roasting.
- **Baste Often**: Use the collected juices to baste the meat every 20 minutes for extra flavor.
Pro tip: For smokier results, finish under the broiler for 2-3 minutes before slicing.

Serve a Buffet That Impresses

With your gyro pan prepped, set up a self-serve station with these additions:
- **Warm Breads**: Pita, tortillas, or lavash in a basket with a small heater pad.
- **Condiments**: Offer tzatziki, guacamole, or spicy sauce in squeeze bottles.
- **Vegetable Sides**: Sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, and pickled onions in small bowls.
- **Garnishes**: Sumac, cilantro, and chili flakes for customization.

FAQ

Can the Vertical Gyro Pan handle different skewer sizes?

The pan’s stainless steel design accommodates standard 3/8-inch skewers. Ensure skewers are securely clamped before roasting.

How long should meat marinate for a gyro pan?

Most recipes require 4–8 hours. Tougher cuts like beef shank benefit from overnight marination.

Can I reuse the marinade?

No—always discard used marinade to prevent cross-contamination. Save some before use for basting instead.
