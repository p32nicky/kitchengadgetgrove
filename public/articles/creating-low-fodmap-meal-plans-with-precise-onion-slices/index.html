Why Precise Onion Slicing Matters on a Low‑FODMAP Diet

Onions are high in fructans, a type of fermentable carbohydrate that can trigger IBS symptoms. Even a small amount can cause discomfort if the pieces are large and release more sugars during cooking. Thin, uniform slices allow you to use the minimum amount needed for flavor while keeping the overall fructan load low. The low fodmap onion slicing technique also helps you distribute the onion evenly across dishes, preventing hotspots of higher FODMAP content.
