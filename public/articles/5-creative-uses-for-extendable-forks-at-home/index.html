FAQ

Are extendable forks dishwasher safe?

Most stainless steel telescoping forks are water-resistant, but because of the sliding mechanism, hand-washing is generally recommended. Dishwasher detergents can be abrasive, and food particles can get trapped in the telescoping joints, making them harder to extend or retract over time.

Can I use these for heavy foods?

These are best suited for light snacks, garnishes, or small pieces of produce. Because the fork is telescopic, it lacks the structural integrity of a solid piece of steel. If you try to lift a heavy steak at full extension, the fork will likely bend or collapse.

How do I keep the telescoping mechanism smooth?

If the fork starts to feel sticky, a tiny drop of food-grade mineral oil on the joints can help them slide more smoothly. Always ensure the fork is completely dry before retracting it fully to prevent any moisture from being trapped inside the tubes.
