For a delicious gluten-free taco recipe, try this:

Gluten-Free Tacos Recipe:

Ingredients:

* 1 lb ground beef or ground turkey

* 1/2 cup chopped onion

* 1/2 cup chopped tomato

* 1 tablespoon gluten-free taco seasoning

* 4 gluten-free tacos shells

Instructions:

1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or ground turkey over medium-high heat, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.

2. Add the chopped onion and cook until it's translucent.

3. Add the chopped tomato and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

4. Stir in the gluten-free taco seasoning and cook for 1 minute.

5. Serve the mixture in gluten-free tacos shells and enjoy!
