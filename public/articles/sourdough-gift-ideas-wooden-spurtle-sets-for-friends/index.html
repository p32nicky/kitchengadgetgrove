FAQ

Q: What is the best type of wood for a wooden spurtle?

A: The best type of wood for a wooden spurtle is dense, hardwood such as beech or maple. These woods are smooth, non-reactive, and easy to clean.

Q: How do I care for my wooden spurtle?

A: To care for your wooden spurtle, simply wash it with warm water and a mild soap. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as these can damage the wood.

Q: Can I use a wooden spurtle with other types of baked goods?

A: Yes, you can use a wooden spurtle with other types of baked goods, such as pizza dough, bread dough, or even cookie dough. The gentle, non-reactive nature of the wood makes it perfect for mixing and scraping a variety of doughs and batters.
