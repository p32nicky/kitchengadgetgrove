Essentials for Your Mini Umbrella-Themed Bar Cart

Gather these materials to bring your tropical vision to life:

  1. Base cart (IKEA Lack or thrifted side table work well)
  2. Paint supplies (neon yellow or turquoise for island flair)
  3. PVC pipe cut to 10-12" lengths for umbrella stems
  4. Mini umbrella bases (plastic or brass)

For the umbrella handles, hot glue PVC pipe sections to the cart’s front edges. Finish with waterproof paint to protect from spilled drinks.

Step-by-Step Assembly Guide

Follow this simple process to build your DIY bar cart mini umbrella small spaces in 2 hours:

  1. Disassemble and sand your cart for smooth paint adhesion
  2. Apply 2 coats of tropical-themed paint (e.g., coral pink + seafoam)
  3. Attach pre-cut PVC stems evenly spaced along the cart’s frame
  4. Mount mini umbrella bases using strong adhesive or small screws
  5. Add a middle shelf using ¾" plywood and L-brackets

Let everything dry completely before adding accessories. For stability, add felt pads to the cart’s bottom feet.
