How to Use Interlocking Corn Holders for RV Camping

Using these holders streamlines every step of your corn prep and eating experience:

  1. Insert shucked corn cobs into the holders just before cooking to keep them fresh.
  2. Grill, boil, or steam cobs securely without worrying about them slipping free.
  3. Stack the used holders vertically in a small container for easy cleanup.
  4. Rinse under water or toss in the dishwasher for quick maintenance.

Pro tip: Freeze the holders briefly before inserting cobs for an even tighter, more stable fit.
