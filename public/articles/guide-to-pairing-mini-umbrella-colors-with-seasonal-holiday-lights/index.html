FAQ

Q: What is the best way to attach mini umbrellas to my drinks?

A: Simply place the umbrella in the drink and adjust as needed. You can also use a toothpick to secure the umbrella if it's not staying in place.

Q: Can I use mini umbrellas in hot drinks?

A: Yes, but be careful not to burn the umbrella. You can also use a heat-resistant umbrella for hot drinks.

Q: How do I store mini umbrellas when not in use?

A: Store them in a dry place, away from direct sunlight. You can also use a small container or box to keep them organized and easy to find.
