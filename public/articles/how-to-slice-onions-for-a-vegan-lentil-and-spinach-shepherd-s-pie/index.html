Pro Tips for Uniform Onion Slices

Even with the right tool, a few tricks help you nail your onion prep:

  • Chill the onion: Refrigerate for 10 minutes before slicing to firm it up and reduce slips.
  • Cut against the grain: For tender layers, slice perpendicular to the onion’s natural fibers.
  • Keep slices consistent: Use the same thickness for all pieces to ensure even cooking in the pie.
