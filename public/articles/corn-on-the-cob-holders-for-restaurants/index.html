FAQ

Are metal corn holders better than plastic for restaurants?

Yes, absolutely. Metal holders are far more durable and offer better penetration into the cob, especially when the corn is fresh and dense. Plastic prongs tend to bend or break after a few uses, whereas high-quality stainless steel lasts for years.

How do I prevent corn holders from getting lost in the dishwasher?

This is a common issue, but it is easily solved by using interlocking holders. Because the pairs snap together, they are too large to fall through the gaps of a standard dishwasher rack. They stay put throughout the wash cycle.

Can silicone handles withstand high heat?

Silicone is heat-resistant and generally handles temperatures well above what you would find in a standard dishwasher or even a hot holding cabinet. However, they should not be exposed directly to an open flame or placed inside an oven while the corn is cooking.
