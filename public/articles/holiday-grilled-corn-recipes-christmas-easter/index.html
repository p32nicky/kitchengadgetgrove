Holiday Grilled Corn Recipes for Christmas and Easter

Here are two seasonal recipes to inspire you:

Classic Christmas Grilled Corn with Herb Butter

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 ears of corn, husks removed
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat (375°F).
  2. Brush corn with melted butter mixed with herbs, salt, and pepper.
  3. Skewer cobs with the Girla Corn Holders to prevent burns.
  4. Grill for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally until charred.

Spicy Holiday Corn for a Festive Twist

For a kick, mix softened butter with 1 tsp cayenne pepper, 1 tbsp honey, and 1 tsp lime juice. Brush onto corn before grilling. Serve with a sprinkle of cilantro and a wedge of lime.

Easter Grilled Corn: Light and Spring-Fresh

Lighten things up for Easter with this lemon-herb version:

  • Replace butter with olive oil.
  • Add lemon zest and dill.
  • Serve with a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

FAQ

How do I prevent grilled corn from burning?

Use the Girla Corn Holders to hold cobs upright and away from direct flames. Rotate occasionally for even cooking.

Can I grill corn without removing the husks?

Yes! Wrap husked corn in foil and grill for 15-20 minutes for a subtler smoky flavor.

Are the corn holders safe for indoor use?

Absolutely—these holders work on stovetops, griddles, or outdoor grills. Heat-resistant silicone handles make them versatile all year.
