Step‑by‑Step: How to Hide Gift Inside Extendable Fork

  1. Choose the right miniature. Aim for something no larger than 0.8" in diameter and 2" long. Good options include a mini‑flashlight, a tiny bottle of hot sauce, or a custom engraved keychain.
  2. Prepare the fork. Pull the fork fully out to its 25.4" length. Use a soft cloth to wipe the interior tube; any debris could snag the hidden item.
  3. Insert the gift. Slide the miniature gently into the hollow shaft. If it’s a bit snug, a few drops of food‑safe silicone spray can ease the fit—just wipe away excess.
  4. Secure the item. Lightly press the miniature against the inner wall so it won’t shift when the fork is re‑extended. For extra security, wrap a tiny piece of tissue paper around the gift before insertion.
  5. Retract the fork. Slowly push the handle back until the fork snaps to its 8.7" compact size. The hidden gift is now sealed inside.
  6. Wrap the prank. Place the folded fork in a small gift box or a decorative pouch. Add a note that hints at a “stretchy surprise” to build anticipation.

When the recipient pulls the fork open, the hidden item pops into view—perfect for a quick gasp and a laugh.

Safety and Practical Tips

While the Extendable Prank Fork is sturdy, there are a few precautions to keep in mind:

  • Avoid sharp edges. The fork’s tines are sharp, so handle the extended fork carefully to prevent accidental cuts.
  • Don’t overload. The internal cavity is limited; stuffing too many items can jam the telescoping mechanism.
  • Test the extension. After you hide the gift, fully extend the fork once to ensure the item doesn’t get stuck.
  • Keep it clean. If you plan to reuse the fork for food after the prank, wash it with warm, soapy water and dry thoroughly.
