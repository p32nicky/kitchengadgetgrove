2 Must-Try Restaurant-Style Grilled Corn Recipes

Elevate your corn game with these restaurant-inspired recipes.

  1. Classic Butter-Basted Corn with Herbs
    • Ingredients: 4 ears of corn, 4 tbsp unsalted butter, 1 garlic clove (minced), 1 tsp fresh thyme, 1 tsp paprika, salt and pepper.
    1. Shuck corn and remove silks. Brush husks with water to prevent burning.
    2. Grill ears over medium heat for 10-12 minutes, rotating occasionally.
    3. Combine butter, garlic, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Brush onto hot corn before serving.
  2. Spicy Lime Crema Corn
    • Ingredients: 4 ears of corn, 1/4 cup sour cream, 2 limes (juiced), 1 jalapeño (finely chopped), cilantro, salt.
    1. Grill corn until charred, 12-15 minutes.
    2. Mix crema, lime juice, jalapeño, and cilantro. Drizzle over corn. Serve with a sprinkle of salt.

Serving Suggestions to Elevate Your Grilled Corn

Restaurant-style grilled corn isn’t just a side dish. Try these ideas:

  • Top with cotija cheese and chili powder for a Mexican-inspired twist.
  • Slip kernels into tacos or salads for a smoky crunch.
  • Serve with a spicy aioli for dipping.

Tips for Perfectly Grilled Corn Every Time

Consistency is key. Preheat your grill to medium-high (350-400°F) to avoid steaming. Use the Girla Corn Holders to keep ears upright and prevent them from rolling. If grilling in husks, soak them 30 minutes ahead to prevent burning. Rotate corn every 2-3 minutes for even charring.
