Essential Safety Tips Before You Begin

Even though the fork is made of polished steel, safety should always come first when kids are involved. Follow these quick checks:

  1. Inspect the fork for sharp edges or loose parts before each use.
  2. Demonstrate the trick with an adult’s hand first, then let the child try under supervision.
  3. Never point the fork at anyone’s face; keep the tip aimed away from eyes.
  4. Store the fork in a protective case when not in use to prevent accidental extension.

With these precautions, the retractable fork becomes a safe, repeatable magic tool.
