FAQ

Do I need to buy all the listed products to host a successful luau?

No. The list highlights versatile items that enhance both drinks and food. Choose the pieces that fit your menu and budget—umbrella picks and straws are must‑haves, while the spurtle set or gyro pan are optional upgrades.

Can I reuse the paper cocktail umbrellas?

They’re designed for single‑use to maintain hygiene, especially with shared drinks. However, you can compost them after the party if you’re eco‑conscious.

What’s the best way to store the mini umbrella straws?

Keep them in a sealed container away from moisture. Since they’re disposable, you can simply toss them after use, but a dry storage box will keep them from getting bent before the event.
