Tips for Maintaining Texture and Flavor

  • Cold onions slice cleaner. Refrigerate the onion for 15 minutes before slicing; the firmer texture reduces tearing.
  • Dry the slices. Pat the sliced onions with a paper towel before adding them to the wok. Excess moisture can steam the veggies and raise the carb content.
  • Don’t overcrowd the pan. Add the onion slices in batches if your wok is small. Overcrowding lowers the temperature, causing the onion to sweat rather than sauté.
  • Season at the right time. Sprinkle a pinch of salt after the onions turn translucent. This draws out moisture quickly, preserving the low‑carb profile.
