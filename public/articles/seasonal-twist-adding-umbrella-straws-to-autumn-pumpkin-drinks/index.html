Serving Tips: Stylish and Functional

Using umbrella straws is more than just a visual upgrade—they’re practical, too. The parasol keeps the straw in place, preventing it from floating freely in thick drinks like hot cocoa or caramel-apple smoothies. Here’s how to make the most of them:

For hot beverages: Insert the straw after the drink is poured to avoid warping the plastic. The parasol acts as a natural handle for easy removal between sips.

For cold drinks: Freeze the straw in the glass (like in a slushie-style pumpkin drink) for added texture and a stable parasol that stays upright.

FAQ

Can umbrella straws handle hot beverages?

Yes, but avoid inserting them directly into boiling liquids. Once the drink cools slightly, the straw’s plastic is safe for hot lattes and teas.

Are they reusable?

Designed as disposable, they’re best used once. However, they can be frozen in drinks for a temporary, reusable effect.

How to store leftover umbrella straws?

Keep the 100PCS pack in a cool, dry place. If they bend slightly, reshape them by gently rolling between your fingers before use.
