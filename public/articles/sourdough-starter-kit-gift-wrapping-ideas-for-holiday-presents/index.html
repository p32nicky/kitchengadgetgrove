FAQ

Q: Can I customize the sourdough starter kit to fit my friend's preferences?

Q: What is the best way to store a sourdough starter kit?

A: The best way to store a sourdough starter kit is in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. You can also store it in the refrigerator to slow down the fermentation process.

Q: Can I make my own sourdough starter kit from scratch?
