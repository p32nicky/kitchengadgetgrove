Preparing a Vegan Mediterranean Mezze with Tofu Skewers on a Gyro Pan

Looking to spice up your plant‑based menu? This vegan Mediterranean mezze with tofu skewers uses a gyro pan with skewer for oven to deliver smoky, caramelized flavor without the meat. The vertical skewer design means you can roast the tofu and veggies in a single pass, saving time and cleanup.

Why a Gyro Pan Works for Vegan Tofu Skewers

The gyro pan’s stainless steel construction heats evenly and locks in juices. The built‑in vertical skewer is perfect for tofu, allowing the pieces to grill on all sides while the pan’s shallow rim keeps excess sauce from dripping onto the heat source. For a vegan Mediterranean mezze, this technique gives a charred edge that mimics classic gyro meat without the animal product.

Ingredients for the Tofu Skewers

  • 14‑oz block firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • Fresh lemon wedges, for serving

Preparing the Mezze Components

While the tofu marinates, assemble the rest of the mezze. A balanced spread of hummus, tabbouleh, cucumber‑tomato salad, and warm pita rounds out the plate. The tofu skewers add protein and a smoky kick that ties the flavors together.

Marinade and Cooking Steps

  1. Press tofu for at least 30 minutes to remove moisture. Cut into 1‑inch cubes.
  2. Whisk olive oil, paprika, cumin, oregano, salt, pepper, and lemon zest in a bowl.
  3. Toss tofu cubes in the mixture, ensuring even coating. Cover and refrigerate 15‑20 minutes.
  4. Preheat the gyro pan with skewer for oven to 425°F. If using a gas or electric oven, place the pan on the middle rack.
  5. Thread tofu onto the vertical skewer, spacing pieces so they’re not touching. Leave a small gap for even roasting.
  6. Slide the skewer into the pan. Roast for 20‑25 minutes, turning once, until edges are crisp and the tofu is golden.
  7. Remove, let rest 5 minutes, then serve over the mezze platter.

Serving Suggestions

Arrange the tofu skewers over a bed of chopped parsley and diced red onion. Drizzle with tahini‑lemon sauce and sprinkle with toasted pine nuts for extra crunch. Pair with the hummus and tabbouleh, and let guests pile pita on their plates. The vertical gyro pan’s design means you can keep the tofu warm by covering it loosely with foil while you finish the rest of the mezze.

Cleaning Tips for the Gyro Pan

After use, let the pan cool, then rinse with warm water and mild dish soap. Scrape off any stuck residue with a wooden spatula. The stainless steel surface resists rust, but avoid steel wool. Dry immediately to keep the pan shiny.

FAQ

Can I use this gyro pan for other plant‑based proteins?

Absolutely. Tempeh, seitan, and even marinated mushrooms roast beautifully on the vertical skewer. The even heat distribution keeps them juicy.

Do I need a special oven to use the gyro pan?

No special oven is required. A standard home oven works fine. Just ensure the pan fits comfortably on the middle rack.

What’s the best way to keep the tofu from sticking?

Press the tofu well and use a light coating of oil in the pan. The stainless steel surface also helps reduce sticking once the tofu begins to brown.
