Mix the Mocktails

Pineapple‑Coconut Cooler

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • ½ cup coconut milk (or coconut‑flavored almond milk)
  • ¼ cup simple syrup
  • Ice

Combine the juice, coconut milk, and simple syrup in a shaker, add ice, and shake vigorously for 15 seconds. Strain into a chilled rocks glass.

Mango‑Lime Spritzer

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup mango puree
  • ¼ cup fresh lime juice
  • ½ cup sparkling water
  • Ice

Stir mango puree and lime juice together, then gently pour over ice in a highball glass. Top with sparkling water and give a quick stir.

Passion‑Fruit Ginger Fizz

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup passion‑fruit pulp
  • ¼ cup ginger‑ale (non‑alcoholic)
  • ¼ cup club soda
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Ice

Mix passion‑fruit pulp with honey, then pour over ice in a coupe glass. Add ginger‑ale and finish with club soda for a light fizz.

Assemble the Flight with Mini Umbrellas

2. Gently insert a 150 PCS Drink Umbrellas Cocktail Pick into the rim of each glass. The pole of the pick doubles as a stir stick, and the paper parasol adds that iconic tropical vibe.

3. For extra flair, thread a fresh fruit garnish onto the pick—think a pineapple wedge on the tiki‑style umbrella, a mango slice on the luau‑style, and a passion‑fruit half on the tropical‑style.

4. Arrange the three glasses side‑by‑side on a decorative tray. The varied umbrella colors create a visual rhythm that invites guests to sample each drink.

Tips for a Polished Presentation

• Keep umbrellas dry. Store the mini umbrellas in a sealed container until you’re ready to use them; moisture can cause the paper to warp.

• Use clear glassware. Transparent glasses showcase the vibrant colors of the mocktails and let the umbrellas stand out.

• Prep fruit garnish ahead of time. Cut all fruit pieces in uniform sizes and keep them chilled. This prevents browning and keeps the flight looking fresh.

• Serve with a straw. A reusable straw adds a sustainable touch and makes sipping easier, especially for the fizzy drinks.

With these steps, your tropical mocktail flight will look as good as it tastes, and the mini umbrellas will become the conversation starter of the night.
