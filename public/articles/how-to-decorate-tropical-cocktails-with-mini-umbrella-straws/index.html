How to Decorate Tropical Cocktails With Umbrella Straws

  1. Insert the straw at a 45-degree angle to secure it in the drink while keeping the umbrella above the rim.
  2. Pair with fruit garnishes, like pineapple wedges or orange slices, for a cohesive tropical vibe.
  3. Use as a base for layered decorations. Slide a paper flower or a maraschino cherry onto the straw for extra flair.

The key is to balance aesthetics and usability—make sure the straw is functional and the umbrella doesn’t drip into the drink. For extra stability, gently press the straw into the syrupy layer of a cocktail if it’s topped with a sugary rim.
