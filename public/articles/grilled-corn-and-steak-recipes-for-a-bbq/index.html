Top Grilled Corn and Steak Recipes to Try This Summer

1. Perfectly Grilled Buttercorn
  • Preheat grill to medium-high (375°F). Brush corn with olive oil and season with salt/pepper.
  • Insert into the Girla Corn Holders and place on the grill, cut side down.
  • Grill 10-12 minutes, rotating occasionally, until kernels are tender and lightly charred.
  • Brush with melted butter and sprinkle with Parmesan or chili powder while warm.
2. Reverse-Sear Steak with Corn
  • Season steaks with garlic, thyme, and olive oil. Place in the fridge for 30 minutes for even searing.
  • Sear steak in a hot cast-iron skillet (1 minute per side) then finish on the grill over indirect heat.
  • Grill corn in the Girla Holders alongside the steak for a cohesive, timed finish.
