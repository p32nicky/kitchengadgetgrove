FAQ

How far in advance should I prep the food?

Aim to have your chopping and marinating done 24 hours in advance. Using tools like a slicing guide can speed up this process significantly, allowing you to store prepped veggies in airtight containers so you only have to focus on the actual cooking on the day of the party.

What are the best drinks for a pool party?

Stick to refreshing, light options. Frozen margaritas, spiked lemonades, and fruit-infused waters are always hits. To make them feel special, add decorative elements like umbrella straws or colorful picks to create a cohesive tropical theme.

How do I keep food safe in the summer heat?

Keep cold foods on ice and hot foods in insulated serving dishes. Avoid mayonnaise-based salads that sit out for too long. For items like corn or skewers, serve them immediately from the grill to ensure they stay at a safe and delicious temperature.
