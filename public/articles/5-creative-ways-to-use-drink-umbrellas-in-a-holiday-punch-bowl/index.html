2. Mini Skewers for Fruit & Cheese Pairings

Turn the cocktail picks into dual‑purpose garnishes. Thread a small cube of cheese, a grape, and a strawberry onto the same stick that holds the umbrella. The result is a bite‑size snack that floats alongside the punch, letting guests dip fruit into the drink or enjoy a quick palate cleanser. 3. Frozen Umbrella Ice Cubes

Take your punch to the next level by freezing small umbrellas inside ice cubes. Fill a silicone ice‑cube tray about three‑quarters full with water, drop a single umbrella into each compartment, and freeze. When the cubes melt, they release the umbrella, adding a playful visual cue without diluting the drink too quickly.

FAQ

Can I reuse the drink umbrellas for multiple events?

Yes, as long as they stay dry and free from food residue. Store them in a sealed container to keep them crisp. If they become bent, a gentle press between two books will flatten them for another round.

Are the umbrellas safe for hot drinks?

The mini paper parasols are designed for cold beverages, but they can handle lukewarm drinks for a short period. For hot cocktails or mulled wine, it’s best to use a separate garnish like a cinnamon stick or orange peel.

Do the picks come with any adhesive?

No, the picks are plain wooden sticks with a small hole for the umbrella handle. They’re ready to use straight out of the box—just insert the umbrella and you’re set.
