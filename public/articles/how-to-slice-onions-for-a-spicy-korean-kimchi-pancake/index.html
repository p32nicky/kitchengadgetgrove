Choosing the Right Onion for Your Kimchi Pancake

For a kimchi pancake, you'll want to use a mild, sweet onion variety, such as a yellow or white onion. These onions will caramelize nicely when cooked and add a rich flavor to your pancake. You can also use a red onion for a pop of color, but keep in mind that it may add a slightly bitter taste.

Additional Tips for Slicing Onions

When slicing onions for your kimchi pancake, it's essential to slice them thinly and uniformly. This will help them cook evenly and prevent them from becoming tough or chewy.

To achieve uniform slices, use a sharp knife and slice the onions in the same direction as the rings. You can also try slicing the onion while it's refrigerated, as the cold temperature will help slow down the enzyme that makes onions tear up when sliced.

Another tip is to slice the onions just before cooking, as they will begin to break down and release their enzymes if left to sit for too long. This will help preserve their texture and flavor.
