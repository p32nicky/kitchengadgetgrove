Now that you have your ingredients and onion holder, it's time to assemble your board. Here's a simple step-by-step guide:

2. Add your crackers and breadsticks, breaking them up into smaller pieces if needed.

3. Use your onion holder to slice your vegetables and fruits.

4. Arrange the sliced items on the board, leaving some space between each piece for a balanced look.

5. Finish off your board with garnishes, such as fresh herbs or edible flowers.
