Marinating Your Chicken for Greek Flair

No Greek dish is complete without a hint of Mediterranean flair. To achieve that authentic taste, marinate your chicken in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and oregano. For the Gyro Pan with Skewer for Oven, try a combination of 1/2 cup olive oil, 1/4 cup lemon juice, 4 cloves minced garlic, and 2 tablespoons dried oregano. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to penetrate the meat.

Assembling Your One-Pan Greek Chicken Souvlaki

Now it's time to assemble your dish. Cut your marinated chicken into bite-sized pieces and thread them onto the vertical skewer provided with the Gyro Pan with Skewer for Oven. Place the skewer into the pan, leaving some space between each piece for even cooking. Add your choice of onions, bell peppers, and cherry tomatoes for added flavor and texture.

Cooking Your Chicken to Perfection

Place the Gyro Pan with Skewer for Oven in a preheated oven and cook for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. Baste the chicken with the pan juices every 5-7 minutes to keep it moist and flavorful. Once cooked, remove the pan from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Tips for a Perfect One-Pan Greek Chicken Souvlaki

To take your dish to the next level, consider these additional tips:

  • Rotate the skewer every 5-7 minutes to ensure even cooking.
  • Use a meat thermometer to ensure the chicken reaches a safe internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  • Don't overcrowd the pan – cook in batches if necessary.
