Step-by-Step Tutorial to Attach Mini Umbrellas

Follow these simple steps to craft your DIY cocktail napkin rings with mini umbrellas:

  1. Trim the parasol stems if they’re too long to sit smoothly on your chosen napkin ring.
  2. Apply a small dab of hot glue to the base of each mini umbrella.
  3. Press the umbrella into the napkin ring, rotating slightly to secure it in place.
  4. Repeat with additional umbrellas, arranging them in clusters for a bold look or single placements for minimalism.
  5. Let the glue cool for 30 seconds before testing the ring’s stability.

Pro tip: Use clear nail polish or waterproof glue to reinforce the parasols if your rings will be exposed to moisture from condensation.

FAQ

Can I reuse these umbrella-adorned napkin rings?

Absolutely! After your event, gently remove any napkins and store the rings in a dry place. The glue holds up to condensation but should be allowed to dry completely before storing.

What if I don’t have a hot glue gun?

Strong craft glue works in a pinch, though it may take longer to set. Avoid water-based glues, which can warp the paper parasols.

How do I store the 150-pack of mini umbrellas?
