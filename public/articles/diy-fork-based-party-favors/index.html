FAQ

Are extendable forks safe for all types of food?

Yes, as long as the food is not too heavy. These are great for appetizers, fruits, and snacks. However, trying to lift a heavy steak or a large piece of corn on the cob at full extension may cause the fork to bend or the food to slip.

Can I use these as actual dinner forks?

Absolutely. When fully retracted, they function exactly like a standard dinner fork. The stainless steel construction makes them durable enough for daily use, though they are most famous for their prank capabilities.

How do I extend and retract the fork?

It works just like a telescopic antenna. To extend, simply grip the handle and pull the tines outward. To retract, push the tines back into the handle until they click into the closed position.
