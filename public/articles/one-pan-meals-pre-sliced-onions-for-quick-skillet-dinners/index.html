When you're tossing everything into one pan, timing is everything. In a stir-fry or a skillet hash, the surface area of your vegetables dictates how quickly they soften. If you have thick chunks mixed with paper-thin slivers, your dish will have an uneven texture.

Uniformly pre-sliced onions caramelize evenly, creating that sweet, savory foundation that ties a one-pan meal together. Prepping Your Onion Station for Success

To keep your kitchen clean and your process fast, set up a dedicated prep zone. I recommend a large cutting board with a rim to catch juices and a separate "discard bowl" for onion skins and root ends. This prevents you from having to stop and clear the board every few minutes.

If you're prepping for a few different one pan meals pre sliced onions are your best friend. Spend twenty minutes on Sunday slicing three or four large onions and storing them in airtight containers. When Tuesday rolls around, you can skip the chopping entirely and go straight to the skillet.

3 One-Pan Dinner Ideas Using Pre-Sliced Onions

Once you have your onions sliced and ready, here are three ways to use them for a quick, delicious dinner:

  • Honey Garlic Chicken & Veggies: Sauté your pre-sliced onions and bell peppers first, push them to the side, and sear chicken thighs. Finish with a glaze of honey, soy sauce, and minced garlic.
  • Beef and Potato Skillet: Brown ground beef, then add pre-sliced onions and thinly sliced potatoes. Cover and simmer until the potatoes are tender and the onions have melted into a savory jam.

FAQ

Can I freeze pre-sliced onions for one pan meals?

Yes! Sliced onions freeze remarkably well. To prevent them from clumping, freeze them in a single layer on a baking sheet first, then transfer them to a freezer bag. You can toss them directly into a hot skillet without thawing.

What is the best way to stop onions from making me cry?

Chilling your onions in the fridge for 15-30 minutes before slicing can reduce the release of the enzymes that cause irritation. Additionally, using a sharp knife creates a cleaner cut, which releases fewer of those irritating gases.

Can the Onion Holder & Slicing Guide be used for other vegetables?
