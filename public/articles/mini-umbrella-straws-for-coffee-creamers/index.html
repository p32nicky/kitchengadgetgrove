Practical Tips for Using Mini Umbrella Straws

To get the most out of your mini umbrella straws for coffee creamers, timing is everything. If you open the umbrella before placing it in the drink, you risk the paper getting soggy if it touches the foam too quickly. I recommend inserting the straw first, then gently sliding the umbrella open once the drink is fully assembled.

For those using hot coffee, be cautious. These are designed primarily for cold drinks, margaritas, and iced lattes. While they can sit in a warm drink for a short period, the paper components of the parasol are not heat-resistant. Stick to iced coffees, cold brews, or chilled creamers to ensure the umbrella stays crisp and upright.

FAQ

Can I use these straws in hot coffee?

It is recommended to use these in cold or room-temperature drinks. The mini parasols are made of paper and wood, which can degrade or lose their shape if exposed to boiling liquids for an extended period.

No, the 100-Pack Umbrella Straws are designed for single-use. Because of the paper umbrella and the material of the straw, they are disposable and should be tossed after one use.

Do the umbrellas fit in standard coffee cups?

Yes, the mini parasols are designed to be compact. They fit perfectly in standard 12oz to 20oz iced coffee cups and glassware without overflowing or blocking the lid if you are using a straw hole.
