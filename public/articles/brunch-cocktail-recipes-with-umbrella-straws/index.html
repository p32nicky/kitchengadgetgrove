  • Piña Colada: Mix 2 oz light rum, 2 oz coconut cream, and 2 oz pineapple juice in a shaker filled with ice. Strain into a glass and garnish with an umbrella straw, a pineapple wedge, and a cherry.
  • Mimosa: Combine 1 oz champagne and 1 oz orange juice in a glass. Add a splash of grenadine for a fruity twist and garnish with an umbrella straw and a slice of orange.
  • Virgin Daiquiri: Blend 2 oz lime juice, 2 oz simple syrup, and 2 oz crushed ice in a blender. Pour into a glass and garnish with an umbrella straw, a lime wedge, and a sprig of mint.
  • Cranberry Spritzer: Mix 1 oz vodka, 1 oz cranberry juice, and 1 oz sparkling water in a glass. Garnish with an umbrella straw, a lime wedge, and a handful of fresh cranberries.
  • Spicy Mango Margarita: Combine 2 oz tequila, 1 oz mango puree, and 1/2 oz lime juice in a shaker filled with ice. Strain into a glass and garnish with an umbrella straw, a mango slice, and a dash of cayenne pepper.

Brunch Cocktail Recipes with a Personal Touch

From sweet and fruity to spicy and savory, here are a few brunch cocktail recipes that will add a personal touch to your gathering:

  1. Strawberry Basil Martini: Mix 2 oz gin, 1 oz lemon juice, 1 oz simple syrup, and a handful of fresh basil leaves in a shaker filled with ice. Strain into a glass and garnish with an umbrella straw, a strawberry slice, and a sprig of basil.
